By M.Hashim | Published Feb 14, 2026 | 6:29 pm

Pakistan’s star batter Babar Azam has spoken about the pressure and emotions surrounding the highly anticipated match against India at the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2026.

Speaking to ESPNcricinfo, Babar described a Pakistan vs India match as one of the most intense contests in cricket. He said the rivalry goes beyond the field and attracts enormous global attention and expectations
